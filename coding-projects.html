untitled project 03



untitled project 03 is a top-down 2d rpg built in Unity. the story follows an overworked and unaspiring bartender, Kay-B, and her day-to-day life until an old friend from college shows up one day. the seemingly coincidental encounter eads Kay-B down to confront her past that was left deeply buried.

in another world with a gun



in another world with a gun is a third person rogue-like rpg. it parodies the isekai trope, where the player is reincarnated in a medieval-fantastical world... with a gun.

iseeu



iseeu is a resnet34 convolutional neural network trained to draw bounding boxes around a human face in a given image. it also provides live webcam inferencing and provides bounding box information for 3rd party use.


[ github ]

nyabit tracker



nyabit tracker is a positive habit tracker/reinforcer, with a tomagachi-style mechanic where the user's mascot's happiness is dependent on how many habits the user has completed. originally made for the universiy of vermont's cs fair 2024.

flo banking



flo banking is an ios/android app designed to help users keep track of the amount of money they spent over a period of a day, week, and month. it also displays metrics on where the user spends the most money. uses the Plaid api.
